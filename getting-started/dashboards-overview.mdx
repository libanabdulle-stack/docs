SkuTrak has **five groups of dashboards**, each designed for a different type of question. Knowing which group to go to saves you time and gets you to the answer faster.

## The five dashboard groups

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Home Pages" icon="house">
    **What they answer:** "How is my account performing right now?"

    Snapshot views across retailers showing sales tracking and supply issues at a glance. This is where you start your week. Free users get a 28-day summary of recent trading.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Performance Dashboards" icon="chart-line">
    **What they answer:** "How am I performing, and what should I do about it?"

    Sales trends, stock levels, and year-on-year comparisons. Use these to replace manual report building, prepare for buyer meetings, track performance against plans, and measure the impact of your interventions.

    | Dashboard | What it shows |
    |-----------|--------------|
    | Top Line Report | Period-on-period comparison across all metrics |
    | Year to Date Report | Detailed YTD breakdown for any metric |
    | Sales Report | Sales trends at category, subcategory, and SKU level |
    | Store Sales Report | Sales trends by store, region, and format |
    | Inbound Service Report | Service levels to identify underperformance |
    | Stock Report | Current stockholding and historical changes |
    | Availability Report | On-shelf availability trends |
    | Waste Report | Wastage trends across product levels |
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Explore Dashboards" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    **What they answer:** "I have a specific question. Let me dig in."

    Flexible, customisable views for ad-hoc analysis. These are your sandbox dashboards for uncovering trends, discovering relationships, and answering questions the standard dashboards don't cover.

    | Dashboard | What it shows |
    |-----------|--------------|
    | Estate over time | Store count and distribution trends |
    | Stores over time | Individual store performance over time |
    | Product Comparison | Side-by-side SKU performance |
    | Metric Comparison | Compare any two metrics together |
    | Time Period Comparison | Same metric across different time windows |
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Supply Dashboards" icon="truck">
    **What they answer:** "Is my supply chain healthy, and where are the risks?"

    Track product flow from depot to shelf. Use these to direct supply chain efforts with daily data, highlight out-of-stocks and ghost stock, surface high OOS-risk products, and identify root causes of availability issues.

    | Dashboard | What it shows |
    |-----------|--------------|
    | Supply Summary | Overview of supply chain health |
    | Inbound Summary | Delivery performance to depot |
    | Latest Forecast | Retailer demand forecasts |
    | Depot Stock | DC stock levels and weeks of cover |
    | Store Stock | Store-level stock positions |
    | Availability | On-shelf availability across your range |
    | Store Availability | Availability by individual store |
    | Promotion Stock | Stock readiness for upcoming promotions |
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Commercial Dashboards" icon="sterling-sign">
    **What they answer:** "What's driving demand, and how can I influence it?"

    Promotions, pricing, distribution, and assortment insights. Use these to understand how commercial levers impact demand, model distribution changes, quantify promotional impact, and spot unexpected metric shifts.

    | Dashboard | What it shows |
    |-----------|--------------|
    | Commercial Summary | Overview of commercial performance |
    | Current Distribution | Where your products are ranged today |
    | Manage Distribution | Model the impact of distribution changes |
    | Promotion Review | What worked, what didn't, and why |
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to choose

| Your question | Go to |
|--------------|-------|
| "How's my account doing overall?" | Home Page |
| "Are my sales up or down, and why?" | Performance > Top Line Report |
| "Which SKUs are driving the change?" | Performance > Sales Report |
| "Is my product on shelf?" | Supply > Availability |
| "Is there enough stock in depot?" | Supply > Depot Stock |
| "Did my promotion work?" | Commercial > Promotion Review |
| "Where am I gaining or losing stores?" | Commercial > Current Distribution |
| "I just need to explore something specific" | Explore dashboards |

<Tip>
  Most users start their week with the **Top Line Report** for the headline, then drill into Performance or Supply dashboards depending on what the headline tells them. The Explore dashboards are for when none of the standard views answer your question.
</Tip>
