A data source is a **connection between SkuTrak and a retailer portal**. When you create one, you give SkuTrak the credentials it needs to log in and collect data on your behalf, automatically, every day.

## Why it matters

Without a healthy data source, SkuTrak has nothing to show you. Every dashboard, every alert, every insight depends on data flowing in from your retailers. A broken connection means gaps in your data, and some retailers only make metrics available for short windows, so you can't backfill what you miss.

## Where to find them

Open the **Data Management** screen using the icon in the top-right corner of SkuTrak. Each retailer you've connected appears as a tile with a status indicator.

## Status indicators

| Status | What it means | What to do |
|--------|--------------|------------|
| **Green tick** | Connection is active and collecting data | Nothing. This is healthy |
| **Red warning** | Connection is failing | Check your credentials. The retailer may have reset your password |
| **Link icon** | No connection set up yet | Click the tile to create a new connection |

## Keeping connections healthy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Update credentials when they change" icon="key">
    If you change your password on a retailer portal, you need to update it in SkuTrak too. Some retailers limit how far back you can pull data, so a lapsed connection means **permanent gaps** in your reporting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Watch for password reset reminders" icon="bell">
    Many retailer portals require periodic password resets. SkuTrak sends you a reminder **seven days before** a required reset so you can update both systems at the same time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Act on failure notifications quickly" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    When a connection fails, SkuTrak notifies the account Owner and Data Source Owner on **day one**. By day three, everyone in the organisation with access to that retailer gets notified. Don't wait for the wider team to find out.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Who can manage data sources

Only users with a **Manager role or higher** can create, edit, or delete data sources. If you need a connection set up and don't have the right permissions, ask your account admin or contact support at skutrak-support@gocrisp.com.

## Deleting a data source

If you need to remove a connection:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Data Sources">
    Open Data Management from the top-right icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the retailer">
    Click on the retailer tile you want to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete">
    Click the bin icon in settings and confirm. This stops all data collection from that retailer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a data source stops data collection permanently. Historical data you've already collected stays in SkuTrak, but no new data will come in. Make sure this is what you want before confirming.
</Warning>
