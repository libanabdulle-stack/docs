Your account settings control **what SkuTrak sends you, how you log in, and which organisation you're viewing**. Most of these are set once and forgotten, but it's worth getting them right from the start.

## Notifications

SkuTrak sends three types of email notifications. You can toggle each one independently.

| Notification | What it does | Who should have it on |
|-------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Data Source updates** | Alerts when a retailer connection fails or needs attention | Anyone responsible for data connections |
| **Daily Performance KPIs** | Morning email with headline sales and supply numbers | Account managers and team leads |
| **Product Mapping Summaries** | Digest of new unmatched products needing review | Anyone managing the Product Catalogue |

To manage these, go to your **profile settings**. A blue icon means the notification is active, grey means it's off. The **Opt out** toggle at the bottom disables all notifications at once.

<Tip>
  Keep **Data Source updates** on even if you turn off everything else. A broken connection means missing data, and you want to know about it the same day, not when your dashboards look wrong next week.
</Tip>

## Profile management

Click your **profile icon** in the top-right corner to access your settings. From here you can:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Change your email address" icon="envelope">
    Update your email and SkuTrak will send a verification link to the new address. The change takes effect once you confirm.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Change your password" icon="lock">
    Enter your current password and set a new one. If you've forgotten your password, use the **"Don't remember your password?"** link on the login page to reset via email.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Switch organisation" icon="building">
    If you belong to multiple organisations (e.g. different business units or client accounts), click the organisation name in the **top blue bar** and select from the dropdown. Your dashboards and data will switch to that organisation's view.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Leave an organisation" icon="right-from-bracket">
    Go to **Settings > Organisations** and click the exit icon next to the organisation you want to leave. You'll need to confirm. If you want to rejoin later, you'll need to submit a request through Suggested Organisations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Password reset

If you're locked out:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Navigate to the SkuTrak login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Don't remember your password?'">
    Enter your email address and submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Click the reset link and set a new password. If the email doesn't arrive, check your spam folder or use the live chat for help.
  </Step>
</Steps>
